About Me


I am a photographer based in London.

My work spans different subjects, including wildlife, aerial, landscape, portrait, and places, but my style is very much rooted in documentary photography. I have a background in environmental studies and sustainable development research, giving me a particular interest in capturing the connections that different people and cultures have with their surroundings. I have a passion for exploring the role of photography in achieving social justice.

I am available for commissions and collaborations involving location and studio portraiture, events, aerial photography, and more. Please email me to enquire about rates and availability.
