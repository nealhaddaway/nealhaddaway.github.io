I am a Research Fellow at the Stockholm Environment Institute in Sweden researching evidence synthesis methodology and conducting systematic reviews and maps.

My main research interests focus on improving the transparency, efficiency and reliability of evidence synthesis as a methodology and supporting evidence synthesis in resource constrained contexts.

I am the co-founder of the Evidence Synthesis Hackathon (www.eshackathon.org). I also co-produced the Stakeholder Engagement in Evidence Synthesis article and webinar series (www.stakeholdersandsynthesis.github.io), ROSES (RepOrting standards for Systematic Evidence Synthesis: www.roses-reporting.com) and PredicTER (Predicting Time requirements for Evidence Reviews: www.predicter.org).
