About Me

Neal Haddaway is a photographer based in London, Stockholm and Berlin.

His work spans different subjects, including wildlife, aerial, landscape, portrait, and travel, but his style is very much rooted in documentary photography. He has a background in environmental studies and sustainable development research, giving him a particular interest in capturing the connections that different people and cultures have with their surroundings. He has a passion for exploring the role of photography in achiving social justice.
