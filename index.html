About Neal Haddaway

Neal Haddaway is a Berlin and Stockholm based photographer.

Neal's main photographic style is documentary, which he explores through a combination of portraiture, wildlife and nature photography.

Throughout his photographs, his primary objective is to tell a story and elicit an emotional response. 'Pathos' is vital for powerful storytelling, something Neal has appreciated during a career as an environmental research scientist.

To find out more about his research, follow him on Twitter.
