According to the world's leading experts and organisations like the United Nations, without urgent political action, society faces catastrophic collapse as the impacts of the climate crisis are now clearly visible in every day life across the globe. At the same moment, in the UK, the right to peaceful protest is increasingly restricted by progressively more draconian policing legislation.

'ProTestDefy' documents ongoing peaceful protests and civil defiance aimed at raising awareness about the crisis and the unethical and corupt inaction by the UK government. This project testifies to what many protesters feel is their last resort in the fight for global environmental and social justice.
